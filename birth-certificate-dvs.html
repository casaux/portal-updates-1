Queensland birth certificates from 1st January 1941 can be verified online. If you have an older document, please choose another type of identification.

You can verify your birth certificate with either the Date of Registration or Certificate Number below.

Certificate numbers are printed at the bottom of birth certificates. If you're having problems finding the right number (which may happen for older certificates) you can also try entering the Date of Registration.


Please enter your Certificate Number exactly as it appears on your birth certificate. This number must be exactly 11 digits in length.

Please enter your Date of Registration below in numeric format; for example 31 01 1980 for 31 December 1980.
